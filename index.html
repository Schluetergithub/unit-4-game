Welcome to Crystal Collector!

Instructions

You will be given a random number at the beginning of the game.

There are four crystals to choose from. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific number of points to your score.

You can win the game by matching your total score to the random number. But you will lose the game if your score surpasses the random number. So choose wisely!

The value of each crystal will be hidden until you click on it.

Each time the game starts, the game will change the value of each crystal.
